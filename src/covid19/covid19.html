Swimming Pools

Regularly sterilize your pool with the highest levels of chlorine (5 mg / liter) and bromine (10 mg / liter) in the water. Sanitize the swimming pool, surfaces, tables and seats after each use and after closing. The necessity to leave at least two meters space between each chaise longue. Pool towels are placed in guest rooms. All group activities banned at swimming pools.

The Rooms

Clean all rooms daily according to the instructions of the Ministry of Health. Clean and disinfect all places of public contact and public bathrooms every hour using disinfectants.
