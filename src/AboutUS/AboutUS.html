About Hostel Postel

Hostel Postel is an online booking site that allows customers to book a lovely vacation on the go no matter what the budget is

Our Story began

We've been inspired by our love for travel and web programming to create a website that will allow people to go on their dream vacations and have a great time without worrying about accomodations and activities to do, and not to forget the delicious food that our partners offer!

Our partners

We started small with only 2 partners and now we have expanded our reach to several countries with numerous partners under our belt.

Meet the Team

We only started with our Co-founders Mira and Amjad and now we employ a small team of 15 people of developers, marketing agents, managers and HR employees, with this small knit family of ours we were able to create a website that we're proud to show you!
